Usage Guidelines

Guided by Respect and Responsibility

We hold a deep respect for the culture, knowledge, and creativity of Indigenous artists and communities.This site has been developed to celebrate the work of Indigenous artists and communities in an ethical thoughtful manner with integrity. We concurrently ask everyone who visits this site to do the same.

Allowed

  • Indigenous art in public spaces with proper documentation
  • Gallery pieces with artist permission
  • Historical art with appropriate context
  • Your own Indigenous artwork with full rights
  • Art with verified provenance and permissions

Not Allowed

  • Sacred or ceremonial art without explicit permission
  • Art from private collections without authorization
  • Images that violate copyright or privacy
  • Offensive, derogatory, or culturally insensitive content

Content Standards

  • Always credit the artist
  • Higl-Quality Image
  • respectful text Standards
  • Acknowledge sources of information

Reporting Issues

If any content that violates these guidelines or contains inaccurate information:

  • Use the "Report Content" button on the art detail page
  • Provide specific details about the issue
  • Include supporting evidence if available

Updates to Guidelines

These guidelines may be updated periodically to reflect evolving best practices and community feedback.
